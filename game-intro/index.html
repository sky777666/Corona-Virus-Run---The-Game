Episode I

Corona Virus Run

Press the ( SPACE BAR ) to move. ( CLICK MOUSE ) 1x on Game screen to play music.

The year was 2020 and turmoil has engulfed the globe. As Luke Myers finishes studying to become a master in his trade a global pandemic spreads across the earth.

Created as the Evil Empire’s ultimate weapon, the Corona Virus, a germ with enough power to destroy an entire planet is now spreading across the globe.

Now it is up to you to avoid becoming down with the sickness. Pursued by the Empire’s sinister agents and waves of virus stricken air, you must race home to deliver plans that can save the world and restore freedom and health to the people of earth…
